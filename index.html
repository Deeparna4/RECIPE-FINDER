Potato fry

Vegetable

Instructions:

**Preparation:** 1. Wash and peel the potatoes. Cut them into thin slices or small cubes for faster cooking. **Cooking:** 1. Heat oil in a large frying pan over medium heat. 2. Add cumin and mustard seeds; let them splutter. 3. Add the potatoes and stir to coat with oil and spices. **Spices:** 1. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well. **Cooking:** 1. Reduce heat to medium-low. Cover and cook for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally. 2. For extra crispiness, cook uncovered for the last few minutes. **Final Touch:** 1. Sprinkle garam masala (if using) and stir. 2. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves if desired. **Serve:** 1. Serve hot as a side dish with rice, roti, or as a snack. **Tips:** 1. Add chopped onions and garlic before potatoes for extra flavor. 2. Adjust spices to taste. 3. Include other vegetables like bell peppers or peas for variation. Enjoy your delicious and simple potato fry!
